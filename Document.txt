1. What is SEO and explain the importance of SEO?
SEO stands for search engine optimization—the practice of increasing the quantity and quality of traffic to your website through organic search engine results. The ultimate goal of SEO for business is to generate organic traffic—searchers clicking through to your website from a search engine results page.

3. What are the Benefits of Using meta tags
By using meta tags, you can make sure browsers and search engines behave as you want them to, and display your content in SERPs according to your preferences. Learn about the most important meta tags for SEO: meta title, meta description, meta robots and what other tags are commonly mistaken for meta tags!
